# Banana Farmer Tower

A Bloons TD 6 mod that turns the Banana Farmer, normally a disposable power that just picks bananas up for you, into a full custom tower with 15 upgrades across three paths and its own Paragon.

![](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Void-n-Null/Banana-Farmer-Tower/main/BananaFarmerShowoff.gif)

I started this in late 2022 and kept coming back to it into 2025, mostly to keep it building against new game versions and to redesign pieces I had outgrown. The Cyborg Farmer got a full visual rework in 2024. It taught me Il2Cpp game modding, custom tower displays, and the reality that most of the work in a "small" game mod is art, balance numbers, and upkeep rather than code.

The three paths, because they are the fun part:

- **The economy path** escalates from "Farmer can now generate bananas" to Banana Banker, Banana Stonks, and finally The Wolf of Monkey Wall Street, which triples the worth of every banana in range.
- **The banana gun path** arms the farmer with slippery banana peels. Upgrades make the peels rotten so they deal damage on hit, speed them up, and eventually let them send MOABs sliding backwards.
- **The range path** is pure escalation: a 10 ft pitchfork, Banan-Occulars, the Banana Phone, and finally the Robo Farmer, a fully mechanical farmer with infinite banana collection range and no arms.
- The Paragon is **Potassium Supreme**, "the embodiment of the monkey economy itself."

Requires [BTD6 Mod Helper](https://github.com/gurrenm3/BTD-Mod-Helper#readme).
