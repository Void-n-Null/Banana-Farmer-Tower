# Banana Farmer Tower

A Bloons TD 6 mod that turns the Banana Farmer, normally a disposable power that just picks bananas up for you, into a full custom tower with 15 upgrades across three paths and its own Paragon.

![](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Void-n-Null/Banana-Farmer-Tower/main/BananaFarmerShowoff.gif)

I started this in late 2022 and kept coming back to it into 2025, mostly to keep it building against new game versions and to redesign pieces I had outgrown. The Cyborg Farmer got a full visual rework in 2024. Most of the work in a "small" game mod turned out to be art, balance numbers, and upkeep rather than code.

The tower has three upgrade paths. One turns the farmer into an economy engine: Banana Banker, Banana Stonks, and finally The Wolf of Monkey Wall Street, which triples the worth of every banana in range. One arms him with a banana gun that throws slippery peels; upgrades make the peels rotten so they deal damage, speed them up, and eventually send MOABs sliding backwards. The last is pure range escalation: a 10 ft pitchfork, Banan-Occulars, the Banana Phone, and finally the Robo Farmer, fully mechanical, infinite collection range, no arms. The Paragon is Potassium Supreme, the embodiment of the monkey economy itself.

Requires [BTD6 Mod Helper](https://github.com/gurrenm3/BTD-Mod-Helper#readme).
